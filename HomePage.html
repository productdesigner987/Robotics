Robotics

Introduction

Robotics is an activity worldwide that people do. In it, you have to build a robot, code it, and then bring it to
competition. There are many types of robotics, but these are two main ones: FLL and VEX.
FLL is most commonly used in elementary school, while VEX is used in middle school.

FLL Robotics

FLL robots are coded by coding blocks and built by LEGO blocks. There is a challenge
every single year that you have to do. 2019's challenge was the City Shaper, where you had to help make the city
smarter using your robot. Each year has the Game part of it and the Innovation part of it, where you have to use
robots and your innovation smarts to fullfill the year's topic. In 2019, contestants were supposed to find a problem
in the city and try to solve it.
