Introduction

Robotics is a fun activity done worldwide. It doesn't matter whether you're in China, or Italy, there would still be robotics. There are many different types of robotics, but most have this simple sequence: build it, code it, practice, practice, and sometimes even bring it to competition. Many different ages participate in this activity, from as young as those in elementary school to those as old as grown-ups themselves. Robotics is a crucial talent used for many things, as robots have a huge role in the world. Computers are part of robotics itself, but most robotics for kids are just for fun and used in competitions. Today you will learn about robotics.

Getting Started

It doesn't matter how old you are or how experienced you are. Everybody can take a part in robotics. All you have to do is choose your type of robotics such as LEGO robots or arduino. You could buy your kit online and start building your design with a few instructions. There are many websites where you can enter your robot to compete. With trial and error, you can build your experience by just taking a few steps to start in robotics. Soon, you could become an engineer trying to build a robot that can go to places no humans can.
