FLL Robotics

FLL robots are coded by coding blocks and built by LEGO blocks. There is a challenge every single year that you have to do. 2019's challenge was the City Shaper, where you had to help make the city smarter using your robot. Each year has the Game part of it and the Innovation part of it, where you have to use robots and your innovation smarts to fullfill the year's topic. In 2019, contestants were supposed to find a problem in the city and try to solve it.

VEX Robotics

VEX robots are coded by coding languages such as C, C+, or C++ and built by VEX parts, which are metal. There is a challenge every season that you have. 2019's to 2020's challenge was the Tower Takeover, where you had to stack blocks and cap towers in order to win points and advance each round. There are many awards for this VEX competition. Engineer Notebooks are for showing how you built your robot and what challenges you faced, and how you solved them. Points are also awarded on these.
